About this space

Upscale, brand new condo in the heart of Leblon. Fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher and washer/dryer, a cute balcony, and steps from the beach, the subway and the best restaurants and shopping in Rio. Every room has AC, ceiling fan and TV.

Living room with open kitchen, bar seating couch and 50" TV, with cute balcony and hammock. Kitchen with dishwasher, washer/dryer, small laundry space, and full size refrigerator. Main bathroom with large shower, towel warmer, all granite. Primary bedroom with an ensuite bathroom and ample closet space. Second bedroom with twin bed and additional pullout, closet and dedicated workspace with monitor, desk, ergonomic chair. High speed Fiber internet, 500Mbps.
